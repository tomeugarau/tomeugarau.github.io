Tomeu Garau

PhD Student at UIB

Hello there! I am Tomeu, I am a PhD student at the Mathematics, Imaging and Learning (MIA) group at the University of the Balearic Islands (UIB). I am also a member of the Institute of Applied Computing & Community Code (IAC3).

I have a B.Sc. in Mathematics from the University of the Balearic Islands and a M.Sc. in Advanced Mathematics and Mathematical Engineering from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). I worked as a Research Technician at the IAC3 from 2024 until 2025, when I got my FPU scholarship to fund my doctoral studies.

Interests

My main research interests currently include:

  • Functional analysis
  • Calculus of variations
  • Partial differential equations (PDE)
  • Numerical Methods
  • Image processing and computer visión
  • Deep learning
